French Omelette

Description

A true French omelette, is just eggs and butter, no filling. The egg is folded for a soft, tender texture.

It's 10% ingredients and 90% technique, so it does take a bit of practice to perfect.

Ingredients (Servings: 1)

  • 3 large fresh eggs
  • ½ teaspoon cold water
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 pinch cayenne or white pepper to taste (Optional)
